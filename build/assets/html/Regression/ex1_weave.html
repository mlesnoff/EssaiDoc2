PCA

Splitting: Tot = Train + Test

The PCA is fitted on Train, and Test will be the supplementary observations.

Here the splitting is provided by the dataset (= variable typ) but, alternatively, data Tot could be splitted a posteriori (e.g. random sampling, systematic sampling, etc.)
